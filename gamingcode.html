How to activate gaming code

When you bought a game, you will have recieved an Email. This Email will include an verification code for steam/Playstation store.

Steam

Step 1: Open steam

Step 2: click on games in the left top corner

Step 3: Activate a product in steam

Step 4: Click next, and accept the terms

Step 5: Copy and paste the code into where it says productcode

Playstation

Step 1: Go to Playstation store and click on your avatar, at the top of the screen

Step 2: Select redeem codes from the drop down menu

Step 3:paste in the code you have, and select redeem

Step 4: The game is now applied to your account
